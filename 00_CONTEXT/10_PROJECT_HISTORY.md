# Project History

## Origin
CIOS evolved from the discontinued `AI_JOB_INTELLIGENCE_AGENT`.

## Why it was discontinued
The previous iteration became overly complex, relying heavily on autonomous web scraping and CRM-like bloated features.

## Lessons Learned
- Autonomous application systems are unreliable.
- Local data ownership is critical.
- Simplicity and maintainability must override complex feature sets.

## Redesign Reasons & Principles
- Shift towards Human-in-the-loop.
- Focus on Executive Career Management as a localized Operating System.
- Adoption of Repository Pattern and Clean Architecture.
